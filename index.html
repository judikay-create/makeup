Highlights

Skin

This plays a vital role in out everyday life, with or without the make-up a good skincare routine is always very needed. Most make-up won't look or stay long if the face isn't well primed or well moisturized.

As a make-up artist you need to understand your client's skin if it's oily or dry, so you can know how to prep it. A good skin brings excellent result for a client's make-up.

Foundation

To achieve any good work for your make-up you need to know your client's shade of make-up before applying it on your client, we have diverse skin color and shade of Foundation and knowing if your client's face goes with oily Foundation or matte makes it better.. And in other to get a more natural look for make-up you must always ensure as a make-up artist that you blend it into your model neck, you don't want to have the face looking different from the neck, sometimes the client might not have a perfect tone; the face might have a lighter shade from the neck or the neck might have a lighter shade from the face.

Concealer

The Concealer is mainly to cover up the undereye or most times to cover up dark spots. We have different shades for different skin colors.. We always use a shade lighter from the person's skin. And the concealer plays a major role in defining our eyebrows.

Powder

This is the solid form of our liquid Foundation. it's help to keep the liquid Foundation from looking shiny and also to help from breakout, some people prefer to just apply the powder even without the foundation most times for personal use.

Setting powder

The setting powder is most times used by professionals to achieve a full face glam by make-up artists.. Its has to be done well still not to have flash back when you snap. The Setting powder can also be used for diverse means. To prep your eyebrows or even oily face and so much more.

Eye and lip

The eye make-up is so sensitive for some because they don't want to lose their eyesight. There are some products in our make-up that can affect the eyes. The eyes also are the main beauty of the make-up, the lashes, the diverse colors and the eyeliner when done properly can transform one's look. The lower waterline, lower lashline, lid for eyeshadow, upper lashline, inner Conner,crease and above crease etc.

Lip

The lip has to be moisturized before applying any lipstick because if the lip is dry it can tear up the Lip.. Before applying lipstick the lip has to be moisturized with lip balm to make it smooth and soft. The Lip is the beauty of any authentic make-up achieved by a make-up artist. We have lipstick pencils, the lipgloss lip look" we also have amazing make-up lip stains of diverse colors, the one that suits the client very well. You can also use the concealer to achieve a well defined and neat lip for your client.

Blush and Highlights

Blush is very essential, and still people don't like it, while others love it. Their make-up routine can't be complete without it. Blush is very essential for any good make-up but too much of it can be very scary and or when not properly done can be terrible to look at. Blush should be part of any good make-up.

Highlights

The highlights help define the client face, it brings out the glowing effect of our model and beautifies the cheekbones, it can also be applied on the bridge of the nose most times on your brow bone or even the tear dot of the eye. You can also apply to the forehead or Cupid bow lips.
